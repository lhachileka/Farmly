Replit Prompt: Extend Farmly Insights Page with Tiered Analytics & Subscription Readiness

Context

Farmly is a live agri-marketplace app with three roles:
	•	Farmers
	•	Buyers
	•	Transporters

An Insights page already exists with basic analytics.
We are building on top of it, not replacing it.

Goal:
Turn Insights into a decision-making engine that adapts to user scale, while preparing for tiered subscriptions.

⚠️ Do NOT remove or break existing insights.
⚠️ Extend current logic and UI.

⸻

🎯 CORE OBJECTIVES
	1.	Enhance Insights with market intelligence, predictive signals, and AI guidance
	2.	Introduce 4 subscription tiers, aligned with user scale
	3.	Keep all features accessible for now
	4.	Implement feature gating logic (flags) for future paywall activation
	5.	Ensure insights are role-aware and personalized

⸻

📊 INSIGHTS FEATURES TO ADD (EXTENSION ONLY)

1. Market Intelligence Layer

Add advanced insights for:
	•	Produce
	•	Livestock
	•	Processed goods
	•	Transport demand

New insights include:
	•	Price volatility indicators
	•	Supply vs demand pressure signals
	•	Location-based price spreads
	•	Best buy / sell / hold windows
	•	Undervalued & overheated markets

All insights must:
	•	Be timestamped
	•	Show confidence level (low / medium / high)
	•	Include a short “Why this insight?” explanation

2. Transport-Specific Intelligence

Extend insights for transporters:
	•	High-demand routes forecast
	•	Route profitability score
	•	Peak transport windows
	•	Idle-risk warnings
	•	Fleet utilization insights (if multiple vehicles)

Buyers & farmers see:
	•	Transport availability risk alerts
	•	Expected transport cost movement

⸻

3. Contract & Relationship Insights

Add analytics related to:
	•	Contract pricing vs live market
	•	Best time to lock long-term contracts
	•	Buyer reliability score
	•	Farmer fulfillment score
	•	Transporter reliability & delay index

These insights support:
	•	Subscriptions
	•	Favourites
	•	Repeat business

⸻

4. Personalized Action Feed (Critical)

Add a section at the top of Insights:
“What You Should Do Now”

AI-generated, role-specific actions:
	•	Buy / sell / wait recommendations
	•	Prepare transport capacity
	•	Adjust pricing
	•	Engage favourite partners

This feed must prioritize:
	1.	Urgency
	2.	Financial impact
	3.	User role

💳 SUBSCRIPTION TIERS (INSIGHTS-ONLY)

Tier 1: Starter (Small Scale)

Target:
	•	Small farmers
	•	Individual buyers
	•	Single-truck transporters

Includes:
	•	Basic market trends
	•	Historical price charts
	•	Personal performance stats
	•	Limited alerts (non-predictive)

⸻

Tier 2: Growth (Medium Scale)

Includes Starter +
	•	Supply-demand pressure signals
	•	Location-based price comparisons
	•	Transport demand heatmaps
	•	Basic AI recommendations (short horizon)

⸻

Tier 3: Pro (Large Scale)

Includes Growth +
	•	Predictive price movements
	•	Best timing to sell / buy / contract
	•	Route profitability forecasts
	•	Contract benchmarking
	•	Early alerts (ahead of general market)

⸻

Tier 4: Commercial / Enterprise

Includes Pro +
	•	Long-horizon forecasting
	•	Bulk & fleet optimization insights
	•	Risk & disruption alerts
	•	Market manipulation detection (sudden spikes/drops)
	•	Competitive positioning insights
	•	Priority data refresh frequency

⸻

🔐 FEATURE GATING (IMPORTANT)

For now:
	•	All tiers have access to all features

But:
	•	Implement a subscription_tier check for every insight
	•	Use feature flags like:
	•	requiresTier: PRO
	•	requiresTier: ENTERPRISE

When paywall is activated later:
	•	No refactor required
	•	Just toggle access rules
