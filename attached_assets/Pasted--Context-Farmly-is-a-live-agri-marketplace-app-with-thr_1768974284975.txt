
Context
Farmly is a live agri-marketplace app with three roles:
	•	Buyers
	•	Farmers
	•	Transporters

Transport already exists but is underdeveloped.
Your task is to fully upgrade the transport role so it becomes reliable, trusted, and revenue-generating.

⚠️ This is an extension, not a rebuild.
Do NOT refactor core architecture.
Do NOT break existing order, escrow, or role logic.

⸻

🎯 Transport System Objectives
	1.	Make transport trackable and accountable
	2.	Protect transporters from false claims
	3.	Reduce delivery disputes
	4.	Enable repeat and subscription deliveries
	5.	Give the app owner full monitoring visibility (hidden)

⸻

🧩 CORE TRANSPORT FEATURES (ALL REQUIRED)

⸻

1. Transport Job Creation (Automatic)

Every confirmed order must generate a Transport Job.

Each job includes:
	•	Order ID
	•	Pickup location (farmer)
	•	Drop-off location (buyer)
	•	Product type
	•	Quantity / weight
	•	Delivery window
	•	Suggested transport price
	•	Job status (open, assigned, in-progress, completed, disputed)

Transport job creation must be automatic and seamless.

⸻

2. Transport Job Marketplace

Transporters can:
	•	View available jobs
	•	Filter by:
	•	Distance
	•	Load size
	•	Route direction
	•	Delivery date
	•	Accept job at suggested price
	•	Counter-offer transport price

Buyers or farmers confirm transporter selection.

⸻

3. Transport Escrow & Payouts

Transport payment must be:
	•	Included in the main escrow OR
	•	Held in a separate transport escrow (linked to order)

Rules:
	•	Transporter payout released only after delivery confirmation
	•	Partial payout allowed for partial delivery
	•	Failed delivery → escrow held for dispute resolution

Escrow logic must protect all three parties.

⸻

4. Proof of Pickup & Delivery (Mandatory)

Transporters must upload proof:

Pickup Proof
	•	Photo of goods at pickup
	•	Timestamp
	•	GPS location

Delivery Proof
	•	Photo of goods at delivery
	•	Timestamp
	•	GPS location
	•	Optional buyer signature

Escrow cannot release without proof.

⸻

5. Transport Status & Tracking (Lightweight)

Implement status tracking:
	•	Job accepted
	•	Goods picked up
	•	In transit
	•	Delivered

⚠️ Do NOT implement live GPS tracking
Status updates are manual but enforced.

⸻

6. Transport Ratings & Reliability Score

Track transporter performance:
	•	On-time delivery %
	•	Job completion rate
	•	Damage or loss complaints
	•	Cancellation rate

Generate a Transport Reliability Score:
	•	Displayed to buyers and farmers
	•	Used for job prioritization
	•	Required for subscription eligibility

⸻

7. Transport Subscriptions (Repeat Routes)

Enable fixed transport contracts for:
	•	Hotels
	•	Schools
	•	NGOs
	•	Regular buyers

Features:
	•	Weekly / monthly routes
	•	Fixed pricing
	•	Guaranteed volume
	•	Auto-generated transport jobs

Linked to:
	•	Buyer subscriptions
	•	Farmer contracts

⸻

8. Route & Load Optimization (Simple)

Help transporters earn more:
	•	Show nearby jobs
	•	Allow multiple jobs on one route
	•	Warn if load capacity exceeded

No advanced optimization required.

⸻

9. Notifications System

Notify transporters of:
	•	New jobs
	•	Job acceptance
	•	Pickup deadlines
	•	Delivery deadlines
	•	Payment release

Notify buyers/farmers of:
	•	Transport assigned
	•	Delivery in progress
	•	Delivery completed
	•	Transport issues

⸻

10. Disputes & Penalties (Transport-Specific)

Dispute triggers:
	•	Late delivery
	•	Missing proof
	•	Damaged goods
	•	No-show transporter

Rules:
	•	False claims → penalty to claimant
	•	Repeated transporter failure → temporary job ban
	•	Disputes logged permanently

⸻

👑 OWNER-ONLY MONITORING (HIDDEN)

Visible only to the app owner.

Metrics:
	•	Delivery success rate
	•	Average delivery times
	•	Transport disputes
	•	Transporter earnings
	•	Failed jobs

Owner monitoring is read-only on dashboard.
Full controls live in a secure admin route.

⸻

🗄️ DATA MODELS (EXTEND ONLY)

Add or extend:
	•	TransportJobs
	•	TransportOffers
	•	TransportEscrow
	•	TransportProof
	•	TransportRatings
	•	TransportSubscriptions
	•	TransportDisputes

Reuse existing:
	•	Orders
	•	Users
	•	Locations
	•	Escrow
	•	Notifications

⸻

🧪 EDGE CASES TO HANDLE
	•	Transporter no-show
	•	Vehicle breakdown
	•	Partial delivery
	•	Buyer cancellation
	•	Farmer delay
	•	Network failure during proof upload

⸻

🎨 UX REQUIREMENTS
	•	Transport dashboard must be clear and actionable
	•	Minimal taps to accept jobs
	•	Strong visual status indicators
	•	Mobile-first design
	•	Clear empty states for new transporters

⸻

✅ SUCCESS CRITERIA
	•	Fewer delivery disputes
	•	Faster order completion
	•	Higher transporter retention
	•	Reduced off-app coordination
	•	Clear accountability for delivery

⸻

FINAL GUIDANCE (IMPORTANT)

Transport is not a support feature — it’s core infrastructure.

Build it clean, enforce proof, lock payments to delivery, and Farmly becomes end-to-end.
