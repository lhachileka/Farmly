Context
Farmly is a live farmer-to-buyer marketplace app with listings, orders, escrow, subscriptions, co-ops, and quality grading.
We are adding demand forecasting as an incremental feature.

Do NOT rebuild the app.
Do NOT refactor core architecture.
Extend existing systems only.

⸻

🎯 Feature Objective

Introduce Buyer Demand Forecasting that allows buyers to:
	•	Declare future demand
	•	Help sellers plan production
	•	Reduce shortages and waste
	•	Increase contract and subscription adoption

This is forecasting, not guaranteed orders (unless converted to contracts).

⸻

🧩 Core Feature Components

1. Demand Forecast Creation (Buyer)

Buyers can create a Demand Forecast specifying:
	•	Product(s)
	•	Estimated quantity
	•	Frequency (weekly / monthly / one-off future date)
	•	Forecast duration (date range)
	•	Preferred quality grade (A / B / C)
	•	Target price (optional)
	•	Location / delivery area

Forecasts must clearly state:
“This is a demand signal, not a purchase commitment.”

⸻

2. Seller Discovery & Response

Sellers can:
	•	View nearby or relevant demand forecasts
	•	Filter by product, quantity, date, and location
	•	Express interest by:
	•	Committing indicative quantity
	•	Proposing price
	•	Suggesting contract or subscription

Multiple sellers may respond to one forecast.

⸻

3. Conversion Paths (Critical)

Enable one-click conversion from forecast to:
	•	Subscription
	•	Fixed contract
	•	Co-Op listing
	•	Scheduled order

No re-entry of data.

⸻

4. Forecast Visibility Rules
	•	Forecasts are visible only to relevant sellers
	•	Priority given to:
	•	Trusted sellers
	•	Nearby suppliers
	•	Co-Op-eligible sellers
	•	Forecasts auto-expire after end date

⸻

5. Forecast Analytics (Lightweight)

Track:
	•	Forecast vs fulfilled volume
	•	Seller response rate
	•	Conversion rate to contracts

Use metrics to:
	•	Improve recommendations
	•	Rank serious buyers and sellers

⸻

6. Notifications

Notify:
	•	Sellers when new relevant forecasts appear
	•	Buyers when sellers respond
	•	Both parties when conversion options are available

⸻

🧠 Intelligence Layer (Rule-Based)

Use existing data to:
	•	Suggest forecasts to repeat buyers
	•	Recommend high-demand crops to sellers
	•	Flag unmet demand gaps

No advanced AI required.

⸻

👑 Owner-Only Monitoring (Hidden)

Only visible to app owner:
	•	Top forecasted products
	•	Demand-supply gaps
	•	Regional shortages
	•	Conversion rates

Monitoring only. No exposure to users.

⸻

🗄️ Data Models (Extend Only)

Add:
	•	DemandForecasts
	•	ForecastResponses
	•	ForecastConversions
	•	ForecastAnalytics

Reuse:
	•	Products
	•	Locations
	•	Users
	•	Contracts
	•	Subscriptions

⸻

🧪 Edge Cases
	•	Forecasts with no seller response
	•	Over-subscribed demand
	•	Buyer cancellation
	•	Seller non-delivery after conversion
	•	Seasonal demand spikes

⸻

🎨 UX Requirements
	•	Simple forecast creation (max 4 steps)
	•	Clear disclaimers
	•	Clean forecast cards for sellers
	•	Clear conversion CTA

⸻

✅ Success Criteria
	•	Increased seller planning activity
	•	More subscriptions and contracts
	•	Reduced stock-outs
	•	No disruption to existing flows

⸻

Final advice (important)

Demand forecasting works only if it’s easy, visible, and non-binding.
Make it feel like raising a hand, not signing a contract.
